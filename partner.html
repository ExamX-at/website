Why Choose ExamX?

Tired of half-baked “BYOD” solutions that just throw you a link? We’ve taken it to the next level. Here’s why ExamX leaves the competition in the dust:

  • Cloud-Powered Security: We harness the robustness of established cloud providers. Don’t like a vendor? No problem—switch to another or even go fully on-prem with ESXi.*
  • Real BYOD, Not a Crummy Shortcut: We provide a complete virtual desktop environment in the cloud, not just a browser link. Teachers can decide which software gets pre-installed—no more wasted local storage or complicated setups.
  • Dynamic Images for All Needs: Schools can simply pick from existing templates or have us build custom images. Admins only see what they actually need—no messy overhead. The more schools on board, the more images everyone can tap into.
  • Traditional Quizzes Welcome: Sometimes you just need a link-based quiz. We support that too—no forced full desktop if you don’t need it.
  • Proven Security Approaches: We didn’t reinvent the wheel—just refined what already works. Trust in tried-and-tested methods that keep your exams locked down.
  • Simplicity for Everyone: Our teacher dashboard doesn’t bombard you with 1,000 random buttons. Creating an exam is literally two clicks—admins can even prep “exam templates” that spin up in seconds.
  • Hassle-Free for Students: They just log in, enter an exam code, and go. Doesn’t matter if it’s a personal laptop or a school-owned device—no AD integration required.
  • Automatic Cleanup: After each exam, our Cleanup Tool wipes every trace of the session. The private device reverts to its original state, as if the exam never happened.
* As of right now only Microsoft Azure is supported. But we encourage developers to implement other providers. The reason we can confidently say that other providers can be used is because of our abstract cloud implementation.
